Privacy Policy

Hall Pass keeps things simple. This page explains what information is (and isn't) collected when you visit and play.

The short version

You don't need an account to play — we don't ask for your name, email, or personal details. Your game high scores are saved only in your own browser (local storage), never sent to us.

Cookies & advertising

Hall Pass is free and supported by ads. We use Adsterra, a third-party ad network, to display them. Adsterra and its advertising partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve and measure ads, including ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • These advertising cookies let Adsterra and its partners show ads relevant to you across the sites you visit.
  • You can learn more and manage choices at Adsterra's privacy policy.
  • You can opt out of some third-party vendors' interest-based cookies at aboutads.info/choices and youronlinechoices.eu.

Games from other sites

Every game on Hall Pass is embedded from another website. When you play, you're interacting with that game's own host, which has its own privacy practices and may set its own cookies. We don't control those.

Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly, aggregate analytics to understand which games are popular. This data is anonymous and not used to identify you.

Children

Hall Pass is a general-audience website and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page. Last updated July 2026.
